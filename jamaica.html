Jamaica's Culture

A vibrant island nation in the Caribbean, has a rich cultural tapestry shaped by its history, music, cuisine, and natural beauty.

Reggae

Internationally renowned music genre pioneered by Jamaican musicians like Bob Marley, known for its rhythmic beats and socially conscious lyrics.

Jamaican Visual Art

Diverse expressions including paintings, sculptures, and mixed media, often reflecting themes of identity, history, and spirituality.

Jonkanoo

Traditional masquerade and street parade during Christmas and New Year's celebrations, featuring music, dance, and folklore characters.

Rastafarian Culture

Spiritual and cultural movement originating in Jamaica, advocating for social justice, African heritage, and a connection to nature.
