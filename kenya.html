Kenya's Culture

Kenya, located in East Africa, is renowned for its diverse cultural heritage, rich history, stunning natural landscapes, and vibrant traditions.

Maasai Beadwork

Colorful bead jewelry and accessories crafted by the Maasai people, often worn as part of traditional attire and cultural ceremonies.

Christianity and Islam

Predominant religions in Kenya, with diverse communities practicing various denominations and sects.

Lewa Marathon

Annual marathon held at the Lewa Wildlife Conservancy, combining sports with conservation efforts and raising funds for community development.

Ngũgĩ wa Thiong'o

Acclaimed Kenyan author and playwright, known for his novels such as "Petals of Blood" and his advocacy for African languages in literature.
