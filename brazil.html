Brazil's Culture

The largest country in South America, is renowned for its diverse cultural heritage, vibrant traditions, stunning landscapes, and influential contributions to various fields.

Samba

Iconic Brazilian music and dance style originating in Rio de Janeiro, characterized by lively rhythms and percussion instruments like tamborim and surdo.

Rio Carnival

World-famous festival featuring elaborate parades, samba competitions, street parties (blocos), and vibrant costumes worn by samba dancers (passistas).

Capoeira

Afro-Brazilian martial art combining elements of dance, acrobatics, and music, developed by enslaved Africans in Brazil.
