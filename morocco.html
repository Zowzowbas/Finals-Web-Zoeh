Morocco's Culture

Morocco, located in North Africa, is known for its rich cultural heritage, diverse traditions, stunning architecture, flavorful cuisine, and vibrant arts.

Gnawa Music

Spiritual music rooted in sub-Saharan African traditions, characterized by hypnotic rhythms, chants, and instruments like the guembri (bass lute) and krakebs (metal castanets).

Zellige

Intricate mosaic tilework, often featuring geometric patterns and vibrant colors, adorning walls, floors, and fountains in Moroccan architecture.

Eid al-Fitr and Eid al-Adha

Islamic holidays celebrated with prayers, feasts, and communal gatherings, marking the end of Ramadan and the Hajj pilgrimage, respectively.

Fes Festival of World Sacred Music

An annual cultural event in Fes, showcasing traditional music, dance, and spiritual performances from around the world.
